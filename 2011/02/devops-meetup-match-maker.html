Devops Meetup Match Maker

February 5, 2011

In the DevOps Survey 2010 I asked if participants attended a DevOps meetup and if they were interested in a local meetup.

141 people indicated that they haven’t attend a local meetup, with majority of them indicating a preferred location. Being an anonymous survey, it makes it difficult to match up people that indicated the same location.

If you want to be added to the list, get in touch with me via the e-mail or TwitterTwitter and provide your contact details and I will update the list. Even if you didn’t participate in the survey, feel free to contact me with your details and location and I will add them.

Also, if you already attend a DevOps meetup, flick me the official website/twitter account and I will add it to the list.
