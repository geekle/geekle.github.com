Devops Survey 2010

December 6, 2010

This survey was created to build a better picture of DevOps in it's current state.

By no means is this a comprehensive survey, but my aim is to capture the current audience and identify the next steps we need to take as a community.

The survey will close 4th January 2011, just after the holiday season.

I plan to release the findings of this survey late January 2011.
